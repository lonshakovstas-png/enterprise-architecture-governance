## Definition

A governance artifact is a document that satisfies all three of the following conditions:

(a) It is classified as a canonical document type: ADR, DOC, REG, or STD.

(b) It is committed to the enterprise-architecture-governance repository.

(c) It carries Status: Approved.

Documents that do not satisfy all three conditions carry no authoritative status under the Enterprise Architecture Governance Framework.

Supporting artifacts — Templates, Registers, and Glossary entries — are not governance artifacts. They are non-authoritative and do not carry binding authority.
