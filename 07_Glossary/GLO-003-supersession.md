## Definition

Supersession is the formal replacement of one approved Architecture Decision Record (ADR) by another approved ADR.

A superseded ADR remains part of the historical governance record and is retained in the repository. It is no longer authoritative and is replaced by the superseding ADR.

Supersession applies only to ADRs.

DOC, REG, and STD documents are revised through versioning and do not use the Superseded status as a version management mechanism.

## Status Vocabulary

### Approved

The artifact is currently authoritative and forms part of the active governance baseline.

### Superseded

The artifact has been formally replaced by a newer ADR and no longer carries authority.

Superseded status applies only to ADR replacement.

### Withdrawn

The artifact has been retired without replacement and no longer carries authority.

Withdrawal does not imply supersession.
